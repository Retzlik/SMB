## DESIGN PRINCIPLES FOR SCREENSHOT INTEGRATION

1. **Screenshots are evidence, not decoration.** They prove the product is real. They should feel like you're looking at an actual interface, not a styled marketing mockup.

2. **Consistent framing.** All screenshots use the same treatment: 8px border-radius, 1px border in warm gray (#E6E5E1), on a stone (#F4F4F1) background. No drop shadows, no device mockups (no fake laptop or phone frames). Just the interface, cleanly cropped.

3. **No annotation overlays.** Don't put arrows, circles, or callout boxes on the screenshots. The capability text next to each screenshot already explains what you're looking at. Let the interface speak for itself.

4. **Retina quality only.** Blurry screenshots undermine the entire "substance disruptor" positioning. If a screenshot is not crisp at 2x, re-capture it.

5. **Responsive handling.** On desktop: screenshot sits beside the text (side by side). On mobile: screenshot stacks below the text at full width with rounded corners. Never cut off or mask screenshots on mobile.
